Our mission

Every person deserves to be seen, cared for and valued.

St Nicolas Homeless Service is committed to being a source of hope, support and empowerment for people without a stable place to call home in East London.

We respond to immediate needs while helping to create pathways toward a more secure future. In every encounter, we recognise the inherent worth of each person.

“We are driven by the conviction that every individual deserves a place to call home and the opportunity to thrive.”
